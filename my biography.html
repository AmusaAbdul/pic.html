EARLY LIFE AND EDUCATION.

Amusa Abdullah Ifeoluwa was born in Odolofa, Ogun State, Nigeria where he lived with is father who is an outstanding farmer. Abdullah was educated at The Fortress College (TFC) which is located at Imosan, Ijebu-Ode, Ogun State. He graduated from the school in year 2021 with 9As in WAEC and got over 250 in his JAMB. He then later got admission to Tai Solarin University Of Education (TASUED). where he is still furthering his education.
